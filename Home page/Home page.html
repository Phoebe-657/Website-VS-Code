Meet our team!

NinetySix Studios is an emerging game development team based in General Santos City, established in 2024 with a focus on creating engaging multiplayer games.

Founded by Kendrick Villaruel, an Electronics Engineer and seasoned Unity game developer since 2015, the team brings a passion for innovation and immersive experiences.

Our latest project, Langit Lupa: Office Series, won the 2024 DOST Esports Game Development Challenge and is currently in active development. We aim to launch it on Steam by the fourth quarter of 2025.

At NinetySix Studios, our mission is simple: to entertain and inspire through every game we create.

Langit Lupa: Office Wars

The fast-paced multiplayer chase game set in an office environment, Langit Lupa is inspired by classic Filipino childhood games where you leap across desks and chairs to avoid the floor and outmaneuver your coworkers. This game aims to bring back the joy and excitement of our childhood, adding a unique twist by playing in an environment we wouldn't expect.
