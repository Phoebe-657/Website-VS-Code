Langit Lupa: Office Series

Short DescriptionFast-paced multiplayer chase game set in an office environment, inspired by a classic Filipino childhood game, where you leap across desks and chairs to avoid the floor and outmaneuver your coworkers.

About this GameLangit Lupa is a playful spin on a beloved Filipino childhood game—think "chase game and the floor is lava," but in a bustling office setting. Jump from desks to chairs, dodge coworkers, and relive the excitement of recess right in the middle of your workday. Fueled by nostalgia and inspired by classic Filipino culture, Langit Lupa brings a carefree twist to the daily grind.

Key Features:

  • Office Meets Playground – Transform ordinary office furniture into your personal safe zones.
  • Classic Childhood Fun – Rediscover the joy of a time-honored Filipino game reimagined with a modern twist.
  • Immersive Environments – Explore and conquer a variety of office-themed challenges that keep you on your toes.
  • Community & Nostalgia – Connect with players who share the same childhood memories and celebrate a slice of Filipino culture in gaming form.

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue developing Langit Lupa—your next office break might just become the most memorable one yet!
